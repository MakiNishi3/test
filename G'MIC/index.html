Privacy policy

The G'MIC Online service uses the images you submit to apply the filters you choose. This is the only personal data it collects. Theses images are deleted 20 minutes after your last interaction with the application.

G'MIC is also available as a free plug-in for the image retouching software GIMP. Download it to get more filters and features than in the online version, at:

The G'MIC plug-in for GIMP
